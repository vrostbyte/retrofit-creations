Build Progress

1 Browse the Store
  1. 1 Open the site. The homepage should load with your logo, the big headline "Built Different. Made to Stand Out." and three matching blue buttons.
  2. 2 Scroll the homepage. You should see category cards, services overview, a community showcase, and customer reviews — all loaded from the database (not faked).
  3. 3 Click Products in the nav. You should see 12 products in a grid with photos or placeholder icons.
  4. 4 Click each category filter chip at the top (Zyn Tins, Automotive Parts, etc.) — the grid should filter to show only that category. Click "All Products" to reset.
  5. 5 Type keychain in the search box — only keychain products should appear. Clear it to show all products again.
  6. 6 Use the Sort dropdown to try "Price: Low → High" — products should reorder.
  7. 7 Click "View Details" on any product card — you should land on a product detail page with the name, price, description, and an "Add to Cart" button.
  8. 8 At the bottom of the detail page, check for "You Might Also Like" — it should show other products from the same category.
Products load, filters work, sort works, detail page loads with correct info.
Blank/empty grid, "error" messages, page crashes, or products from wrong categories show up after filtering.
2 Shopping Cart
  1. 1 On the Products page, click "Add to Cart" on any product. A cart drawer should slide in from the right showing that item.
  2. 2 The shopping cart icon in the top-right corner should now show a blue circle with 1 on it.
  3. 3 Add 2 more products from different categories. The badge should update to 3.
  4. 4 In the cart drawer, click the + button on any item — the quantity should go up and the total at the bottom should update.
  5. 5 Click the − button to reduce quantity. When quantity is 1, the − button should be grayed out (can't go below 1).
  6. 6 Click the × (remove) button on one item — it should disappear from the cart and the total should update.
  7. 7 Refresh the page. Click the cart icon again. Your remaining items should still be there — the cart saves in your browser automatically.
  8. 8 Press Escape on your keyboard (or click "Continue Shopping" or the backdrop) — the drawer should close smoothly.
  9. 9 Click "Checkout" at the bottom of the drawer — you should navigate to /checkout.
Cart drawer opens, quantities update, totals are correct, cart survives page refresh, drawer closes cleanly.
Cart is always empty after adding items, badge stays at 0, drawer doesn't open, items disappear after refresh.

4 Account Registration & Login
  1. 1 Click the person icon in the top-right corner (or go to /account/login).
  2. 2 Click "Create one" at the bottom. Create a test account with a NEW email you haven't used before. Fill in: full name, email, password (8+ characters).
  3. 3 Click "Create Account" — you should see a "Check Your Email" screen telling you to confirm your address.
  4. 4 Open that email inbox. Find the confirmation email and click the confirmation link. You should be brought back to the site.
  5. 5 Log in with the new account. After logging in, the person icon should be replaced with your initials in a blue circle (e.g., "HB" for Hailie B.).
  6. 6 Click your initials — a dropdown should appear with: "My Account" and "Sign Out".
  7. 7 Click "Sign Out" — you should be taken to the homepage. The initials should disappear and the person icon returns.

5 Admin Panel

Log in as your admin account first, then go to /admin.

Dashboard

  1. 1 The dashboard should show stat cards: Active Products, New Quotes, New Orders, Pending Reviews — with correct numbers.
  2. 2 The test order you placed in Part 3 should appear in the "Recent Orders" panel.

Products

  1. 1 Go to Admin → Products. All 12 products should be listed in a table.
  2. 2 Click the green toggle next to any product to deactivate it (turns gray). Open /products in a new tab — that product should be gone.
  3. 3 Toggle it back on. Refresh the products page — it should reappear.
  4. 4 Click the ★ star to mark a product as "Featured". Refresh the homepage — it should appear in the community/product sections.
  5. 5 Click "Edit" on any product. Change the price by $1 and click "Save Changes". Verify the new price shows on the product page.
  6. 6 Click "Add Product". Fill in: name, price, category. Click Create. The new product should appear in the table and on the store.

Orders

  1. 1 Go to Admin → Orders. Your test order should appear with status "received".
  2. 2 Change the status to "Processing" and click Save. The status label should update.
  3. 3 Change status to "Shipped", enter a fake tracking number, and save. Verify it saves.

Testimonials & Community

  1. 1 Go to Admin → Testimonials. You should see 8 pre-loaded approved reviews. Click "Delete" on one — it should disappear from /testimonials.
  2. 2 Go to Admin → Community. You should see 6 pre-loaded posts. Click "Mark Featured" on one — it should show ★ Featured.
All changes save correctly and immediately reflect on the public-facing pages.
Save shows an error, changes don't appear on the public site, page crashes after saving.

! If Something Breaks — What to Tell Josh
